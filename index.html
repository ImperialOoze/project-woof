The Newfoundland

Origin

Disregarding it's peculiar name, this breed is renowned as one of the best work dogs. They are typically used to rescue humans, herder and a sled dog. Unsuprisingly, they originate from the island of Newfoundland and was then transported to England in the 1800's.

Interesting Facts

  • A Landseer Newfoundland holds the World Record for the tallest dog.
  • Newfoundlands gain over 100 pounds in their first year.
  • They may not look it but these dog love to be in the water. They even have webbed feet.

Before you Buy

Life Span 8-10 Years
Grooming High Maintenance
Aggression Low
Friendliness High

How to Handle

Newfoundlands are naturally kind but be sure to take him/her with you to many places when they are puppies so they do not become scared/hostile when going to new places. This also makes them social.

Make sure to train them when young so you don't have a 125 lb beast you can't control.

If you got your puppy from a breeder, make sure you feed him the same foods until adulthood to minimize digestive upsets.
